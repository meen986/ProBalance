Business Entity Registration:
  • Assist clients in choosing the right business structure (sole proprietorship, partnership, LLP, private limited, etc.).
  • Facilitate the registration process with the appropriate regulatory authorities.
Goods and Services Tax (GST) Registration:
  • Guide clients through the GST registration process.
  • Ensure compliance with GST regulations and provide ongoing support.
Professional Tax Registration:
  • Help clients register for professional tax, which is applicable in certain states.
  • Assist in complying with state-specific professional tax regulations.
Shop and Establishment License:
  • Facilitate the obtaining of shop and establishment licenses required for businesses operating in specific locations.
Import-Export Code (IEC):
  • Assist clients in obtaining Import-Export Codes for businesses involved in international trade.
Trade License:
  • Help businesses obtain trade licenses from local municipal authorities.
  • Ensure compliance with local regulations for operating specific types of businesses.
Food License (FSSAI):
  • Guide clients through the process of obtaining Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) licenses for food-related businesses.
Environmental Clearances:
  • Assist clients in obtaining environmental clearances and licenses for businesses that may impact the environment.
MSME Registration:
  • Facilitate registration under the Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) Act to avail of benefits provided to small businesses.
Trademark and Copyright Registration:
  • Provide guidance on protecting intellectual property through trademark and copyright registration.
PAN and TAN Registration:
  • Assist in obtaining Permanent Account Numbers (PAN) and Tax Deduction and Collection Account Numbers (TAN) for businesses.
Labour Welfare Fund Registration:
  • Help businesses register for labor welfare funds as required by state labor laws.
Other Industry-Specific Licenses:
  • Depending on the industry, assist in obtaining specific licenses required for regulatory compliance.
Renewals and Compliance Management:
  • Monitor and manage the renewal of licenses to ensure continuous compliance with regulatory requirements.
