Tax Compliance:
  • Prepare and file income tax returns for individuals, businesses, and entities.
  • Ensure compliance with Goods and Services Tax (GST) regulations, including return filing and reconciliation.
Statutory Audits:
  • Conduct statutory audits in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Prepare and submit audit reports to regulatory authorities.
Internal Audits:
  • Conduct internal audits to assess and improve the effectiveness of internal controls.
  • Identify areas for process improvement and risk mitigation.
Company Law Compliance:
  • Assist in maintaining compliance with the Companies Act, including annual filings and board meeting documentation.
  • Handle filings related to changes in the company's structure, directors, or registered office.
ROC (Registrar of Companies) Filings:
  • Facilitate filings with the ROC, such as annual returns, financial statements, and other required documents.
XBRL Filings:
  • Prepare and file financial statements in eXtensible Business Reporting Language (XBRL) format as required by regulatory authorities.
Compliance Certifications:
  • Provide compliance certifications required under various laws and regulations.
Secretarial Services:
  • Offer secretarial services, including drafting minutes of meetings, maintaining statutory records, and ensuring compliance with secretarial standards.
Labour Law Compliance:
  • Ensure compliance with labor laws, including timely filings of returns and compliance with statutory requirements related to employees.
Environmental Compliance:
  • Assist in complying with environmental regulations, including obtaining and renewing necessary permits.
Import-Export Compliance:
  • Facilitate compliance with import-export regulations, including documentation and filings with customs authorities.
Legal Compliance Audits:
  • Conduct legal compliance audits to assess adherence to various laws applicable to the business.
Annual Compliance Calendar:
  • Develop and maintain an annual compliance calendar for clients to track key filing deadlines and regulatory requirements.
Due Diligence:
  • Conduct due diligence to assess the compliance status of a business before mergers, acquisitions, or investments.
Insurance Compliance:
  • Ensure compliance with insurance regulations and filings required for insurance coverage.
