TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) Services:

  • TDS Compliance and Consulting: Assist clients in understanding TDS provisions applicable to their transactions and guide them in complying with TDS requirements.
  • TDS Return Filing: Prepare and file TDS returns accurately and within deadlines on behalf of your clients.
  • TDS Audit and Review: Conduct audits and reviews to ensure that your clients are deducting TDS correctly and maintaining proper documentation.
  • TDS Advisory: Provide ongoing advisory services to keep clients informed about changes in TDS regulations and help them optimize TDS deductions.
  • Vendor and Employee TDS Compliance: Assist clients in ensuring compliance with TDS regulations when making payments to vendors, contractors, and employees.
  • TDS Certificates Issuance: Generate and issue TDS certificates (Form 16, Form 16A) to deductees on behalf of your clients.

TCS (Tax Collected at Source) Services:

  • TCS Compliance and Consulting: Advise clients on TCS provisions relevant to their business and help them comply with TCS requirements.
  • TCS Return Filing: Prepare and file TCS returns accurately and within stipulated time frames on behalf of your clients.
  • TCS Audit and Review: Conduct audits and reviews to ensure that your clients are correctly collecting TCS and maintaining proper records.
  • TCS Advisory: Keep clients updated on changes in TCS regulations and provide advisory services for optimal TCS collection.
  • Vendor and Customer TCS Compliance: Assist clients in complying with TCS regulations when selling specified goods or services to customers.
  • TCS Certificates Issuance: Generate and issue TCS certificates to customers as required by tax regulations.
